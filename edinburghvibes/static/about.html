About Me

Hello, I’m Alfa Yohannis, a dedicated and passionate photographer based in Gading Serpong area of Tangerang, Indonesia.

My main interest lies in portrait photography, such as graduation sessions and selfies, but I also enjoy capturing the artistry of food, the beauty of landscapes, and more.

I am available for bookings in Jakarta and Tangerang. My competitive packages start at just 250,000 IDR and include three basic edited photos. Additionally, all raw images will be shared with you, ensuring you have full access to your collection.

If you’re looking for a portrait session, product or food photography, or have other photography needs, please feel free to contact me to discuss or book a session!
